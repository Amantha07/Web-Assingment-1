Thuruliya Volunteer

Thuruliya Volunteers is the platform for those who wish to donate their valuable time and skills to build a sustainable country to the world. Volunteers are vital to our mission, and this platform provides a space for a committed and passionate individual to be part of this nature-saving work. To date, Thuruliya has over 70 registered volunteers who are focused on the in Sri Lanka. Our dedicated volunteers engage in a variety of initiatives that support the services Thuruliya contribute planting and protecting projects, Education and awareness, Natural Products promoting projects.
